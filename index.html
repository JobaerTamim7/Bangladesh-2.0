Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh is fostering a culture of innovation through technology hubs, startups, and research initiatives, driving progress in sectors like IT, agriculture, and healthcare.

Sustainability

With a focus on renewable energy, eco-friendly practices, and climate resilience, Bangladesh is committed to building a sustainable future for generations to come.

Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure projects, including highways, bridges, and smart cities, are transforming Bangladesh into a hub of connectivity and economic growth.

Empowerment

Empowering communities through education, skill development, and gender equality initiatives is key to unlocking the full potential of Bangladesh's people.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation
Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.
Green Energy
Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.
Modern Infrastructure
Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

It's the work of millions of voices, almost the voices of the entire nation, voices that have demanded change, voices that continue to push us all to build a future based on human rights, freedom of speech, justice, and inclusion. It is more so now, as the aspirations of the revolution are fresh in the minds. Let's challenge each other, listen to each other, and dare to imagine a new world with a new lifestyle consistent with an environmentally safe planet. And build an economy where fruits of technology, economic growth are shared evenly by all people, not monopolised by the privileged few.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
